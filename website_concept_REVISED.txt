## 6. Design Principles

### Visual Identity
- **Primary Color:** Indigo (#4f46e5 to #6366f1) - more academic, less commercial than purple
- **Background:** Light grays (#f8fafc, #e2e8f0)
- **Text:** Dark grays for readability (#2d3748, #4a5568, #64748b)

### Typography
- **Font:** Inter (modern, professional, readable)
- **Headings:** Bold, clear hierarchy
- **Body:** 1.6-1.7 line height for academic readability

### Tone
- **Professional but approachable**
- **Evidence-based** (cite actual numbers from CV)
- **Academic rather than commercial**
- **Collaborative not sales-focused**

### Content Guidelines
- Use "research collaboration" not "hire me"
- Use "projects" not "clients"
- Cite specific universities and real work
- Avoid marketing hyperbole
- Focus on methodology and skills
- Be specific about domain expertise
